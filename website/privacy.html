FlowSwitch privacy summary

Privacy

FlowSwitch is designed as a local-first desktop tool for Windows workspace switching.

Local data

Profiles, layout metadata, and related configuration are intended to live on your machine under %APPDATA%/FlowSwitch.

System integration

To capture monitor layouts and launch apps, FlowSwitch reads OS-provided information and interacts with local processes.

No required account

The app does not require a cloud account to perform its core workspace capture and switching functions.

Diagnostics

Optional local logs may exist for troubleshooting, including the latest launch diagnostics path documented in the repository.
