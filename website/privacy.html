Privacy

This page summarizes what the FlowSwitch desktop application is designed to access on your PC. Replace the bracketed sections with your legal entity, contact method, and data practices before you market broadly.

Local data

FlowSwitch stores profiles, layout metadata, and related configuration on your machine as part of normal operation. That data stays under your user account unless you export it or sync it through a feature you add later.

System integration

To capture monitors, enumerate installed applications, and launch programs, the app reads OS-provided information (for example registry and shell paths on Windows) and interacts with running processes. It does not need an account to perform those functions.

Diagnostics

Optional launch diagnostics may be written to a local log path during development or troubleshooting. Review your product’s logging behavior and describe it here, including retention and how users can disable it.
