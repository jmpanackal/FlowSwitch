FlowSwitch release history

Changelog

High-level release notes for FlowSwitch.

v0.1.3 early access May 2026
  • Launch associated files and folders with a clearer timeline, post-run summary (paths with icons), and improved Explorer handling including multi-folder tabs.
  • Apps catalog hygiene (noise filtering, scan roots), optional user-provided executables, and content-library snackbars.
  • Content inspector layout, associated-content caps, launch follow-ups, and related fixes.
v0.1.2 early access April 2026
  • Profile header: icon and type shortcuts, clearer launch meta tooltips, and stable launch button sizing.
  • Profile icons: more presets, optional custom image from disk, and safer persistence when saving profiles.
  • Title bar and library chrome refinements (compact controls, Flow library toolbar).
v0.1.1 early access April 2026
  • Profile shell access, recent launches, and related preferences plumbing.
  • Pre-launch app behavior (in-profile and outside apps) with optional per-app narrowing.
  • Profile settings UX: accordion scope panels, icons, and clearer default vs limited states.
v0.1.0 early access April 2026
  • Initial public Windows packaging with installer and portable executable.
  • Launch, monitor layout, and profile switching baseline.
  • Marketing site download metadata wired to the release payload.
